Silicon Valley Business Connections

“where Your Success comes from your Connections”

KDAW  122O AM

Saturday 2:30PM

Starting Saturday March 25th @ 2:30pm on KDOW 1220AM, our president, Carl Davis, Jr. along with Producer and Co-Host Karl Welch "Big Pappa" will air their 1st show called Silicon Valley Business Connections (SVBC), "Where Your Success comes from your Connections."

 

SVBC is a show where the “Rock Stars” of Business, Education, Politics, Philanthropy and Technology come together to share what connections led to their success.

 

This is a platform for listeners to hear the stories, challenges, and the successes of those who are making it, and those who've made an impact on Silicon Valley.

 

Silicon Valley Business Connections is about sharing the success and challenges facing those who are making an impact or who’ve made an impact onto Silicon Valley. Remember, what happens in Silicon Valley impacts the world. We interview influencers and pioneers that have impacted Silicon Valley.

Out of the Bay Area? Listen Live over the Internet over at SalemInteractiveMedia. If miss show then go to blackchamber.com under Radio Show and see all the previous recorded shows via Podcast!

Our Mission:

To create, identify and/or expand economic development opportunities for all small businesses, especially African American and minority businesses in Silicon Valley.
